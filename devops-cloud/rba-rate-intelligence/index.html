- P = Principal (loan amount)
- r = Monthly rate (annual rate √∑ 12 √∑ 100)

RBA's Inflation Target

  • Target band: 2-3% annual inflation
  • Above 3%: RBA may raise rates to cool economy
  • Below 2%: RBA may cut rates to stimulate growth
